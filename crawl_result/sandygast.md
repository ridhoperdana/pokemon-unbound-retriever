# Sandygast
In: [Pokédex](https://unboundwiki.com/pokemon/sandygast/<https:/unboundwiki.com/category/pokedex/>)
Sandygast  
---  
![](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/sprites/pokemon/sandygast.png)  
Type | Ghost Ground  
National Dex | #769  
Location | 
  * Route 3 (Sand Encounter, Monday-Tuesday)
  * Dehara Beach (Sand Castle Encounter, Wednesday and Sunday)

  
Abilities | Water Compact | Water-type hits raise Defense.  
Sand Veil | Ups evasion in a sandstorm.  
Egg Groups | Amorphous  
Wild Held Items | Spell Tag (5% chance)  
## Location
### (click to expand)
Prerequisites  
---  
  * Clear the Icicle Cave section of the game’s starting area

  
Sandygast is found in two locations in Pokemon Unbound, though its appearance is always in the same place. Additionally, it spawns only on certain days for each location:
  * **Route 3** – Mondays, Tuesdays
  * **Dehara Beach** – Sundays, Wednesdays


### Route 3 (Mondays and Tuesdays)
To find Sandygast in the early game, go south from Flower Paradise’s Fly location.
![Going south from the Flower Paradise central area. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/01-pokemon-unbound-going-south-from-the-flower-paradise-central-area.jpg)Going south from the Flower Paradise central area.
Continue south to exit Flower Paradise and enter Route 3.
![Leaving Flower Paradise. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/02-pokemon-unbound-leaving-flower-paradise.jpg)Leaving Flower Paradise.
Go down the stairs, then turn west to find a sand castle. 
Interact with it to fight Sandygast. The sand castle Pokemon only spawns here on Mondays and Tuesdays.
![Approaching the Route 3 Sandygast. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/03-pokemon-unbound-approaching-the-route-3-sandygast.jpg)Approaching the Route 3 Sandygast.
### Dehara Beach (Sundays and Wednesdays)
To find a stronger Sandygast, fly to the Dehara Town Pokemon Center and go north.
![Going north from the Dehara City Pokemon Center. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/04-pokemon-unbound-going-north-from-the-dehara-city-pokemon-center.jpg)Going north from the Dehara City Pokemon Center.
Continue north as you walk past the entrance to Rift Cave.
![Continuing north after walking past Rift Cave. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/05-pokemon-unbound-continuing-north-after-walking-past-rift-cave.jpg)Continuing north after walking past Rift Cave.
You’ll eventually reach Dehara Beach.
![Reaching Dehara Beach. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/06-pokemon-unbound-reaching-dehara-beach.jpg)Reaching Dehara Beach.
On Sundays and Wednesdays, two sand castles spawn on Dehara Beach, and interacting with them prompts Sandygast to attack.
![Approaching one of the Sandygasts on Dehara Beach. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/07-pokemon-unbound-approaching-1-of-the-sandygasts-on-dehara-beach.jpg)Approaching one of the Sandygasts on Dehara Beach.
