# Archen
In: [Pokédex](https://unboundwiki.com/pokemon/archen/<https:/unboundwiki.com/category/pokedex/>)
Archen  
---  
![](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/sprites/pokemon/archen.png)  
Type | Rock Flying  
National Dex | #566  
Location | [Revive](https://unboundwiki.com/pokemon/archen/<https:/unboundwiki.com/misc-info/useful-npcs/fossil-reviver/>) a Plume Fossil  
Abilities | Defeatist | Gives up at half HP.  
Klutz | Can't use items.  
Egg Groups | Flying, Water 3  
Wild Held Items | None  
**Tip:** The Plume Fossil for Archen does not seem to drop often from Rock Smash. So the better way to get this fossil is to [mine underwater](https://unboundwiki.com/pokemon/archen/<https:/unboundwiki.com/items/mining-items/>).
