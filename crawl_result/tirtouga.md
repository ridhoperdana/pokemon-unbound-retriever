# Tirtouga
In: [Pokédex](https://unboundwiki.com/pokemon/tirtouga/<https:/unboundwiki.com/category/pokedex/>)
Tirtouga  
---  
![](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/sprites/pokemon/tirtouga.png)  
Type | Water Rock  
National Dex | #564  
Location | [Revive](https://unboundwiki.com/pokemon/tirtouga/<https:/unboundwiki.com/misc-info/useful-npcs/fossil-reviver/>) the Cover Fossil  
Abilities | Filter | Weakens super effective moves.  
Solid Rock | Negates 1-hit KO attacks.  
Swift Swim | Raises Speed in rain.  
Egg Groups | Water 1, Water 3  
Wild Held Items | None  
**Tip:** The Cover Fossil for Tirtouga does not seem to drop often from Rock Smash. So the better way to get this fossil is to [mine underwater](https://unboundwiki.com/pokemon/tirtouga/<https:/unboundwiki.com/items/mining-items/>).
