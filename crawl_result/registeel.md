# Registeel
In: [Pokédex](https://unboundwiki.com/pokemon/registeel/<https:/unboundwiki.com/category/pokedex/>)
Registeel  
---  
![](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/sprites/pokemon/registeel.png)  
Type | Steel  
National Dex | #379  
Location | Inside the Route 18 Island Cave  
Abilities | Clear Body | Prevents ability reduction.  
Filter | Weakens super effective moves.  
Egg Groups | Undiscovered  
Wild Held Items | None  
## Location
### (click to expand)
Prerequisites  
---  
  * Complete the [“Tomb Raider”](https://unboundwiki.com/pokemon/registeel/<https:/unboundwiki.com/missions/mission-076/>) side mission
  * Defeat the Elite Four and become Champion

  
Fly to the Route 18 Victory Road Pokemon Center. Then use Surf to go south.
![Going south from the Route 18 Pokemon Center. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/01-pokemon-unbound-going-south-from-the-route-18-pokemon-center.jpg)Going south from the Route 18 Pokemon Center.
Descend from the waterfall and continue south. 
Swim past some rocks to your west, then turn left and follow that path west, surfing past the rocks.
![Following the path south of the rocks. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/02-pokemon-unbound-following-the-path-south-of-the-rocks.jpg)Following the path south of the rocks.
Continue west and you’ll see the entrance to the Island Cave that you want to enter.
![Entering the Island Cave. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/03-pokemon-unbound-entering-the-island-cave.jpg)Entering the Island Cave.
Inside is a crystal puzzle. 
Interacting with a crystal causes it to change colors, and the goal is to make every crystal red.
![The crystal puzzle players must solve. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/04-pokemon-unbound-the-crystal-puzzle-players-must-solve.jpg)The crystal puzzle players must solve.
Follow the steps below after entering the cave to solve the puzzle. If you make a mistake along the way, exit and re-enter the cave to reset the puzzle and start from the first step.
![The position of every crystal in the room. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/05-pokemon-unbound-the-position-of-every-crystal-in-the-room.jpg)The position of every crystal in the room.
  1. Interact with the **center rock three times** so it’s purple
  2. Interact with the **west rock one time** so it’s purple
  3. Interact with the **northwest rock two times** so it’s purple
  4. Interact with the **southeast rock one time** so it’s purple
  5. Interact with the **southwest rock two times** so it’s blue
  6. Interact with the **center rock two times** so it’s purple
  7. Interact with the **southeast rock two times** so it’s blue
  8. Interact with the **center rock three times** so every rock turns red


Solving the puzzle will open up a path to the north.
![Entering the new path after solving the puzzle. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/06-pokemon-unbound-entering-the-new-path-after-solving-the-puzzle.jpg)Entering the new path after solving the puzzle.
Registeel is waiting inside the newly opened area.
![Finding Registeel. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/07-pokemon-unbound-finding-registeel.jpg)Finding Registeel.
