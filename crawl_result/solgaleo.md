# Solgaleo
In: [Pokédex](https://unboundwiki.com/pokemon/solgaleo/<https:/unboundwiki.com/category/pokedex/>)
Solgaleo  
---  
![](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/sprites/pokemon/solgaleo.png)  
Type | Psychic Steel  
National Dex | #791  
Location | 
  * Evolve Cosmoem at Level 53 during the day
  * Route 12 Beach Portal during the day (post-game) (with [Lunala](https://unboundwiki.com/pokemon/solgaleo/<https:/unboundwiki.com/pokemon/lunala/>) in party)

  
Abilities | Full Metal Body | Prevents ability reduction.  
Battle Armor | Blocks critical hits.  
Egg Groups | Undiscovered  
Wild Held Items | None  
## Location
### (click to expand)
Prerequisites  
---  
  1. Be in the post-game
  2. Start the [Portal Purge mission](https://unboundwiki.com/pokemon/solgaleo/<https:/unboundwiki.com/missions/mission-050/>)
  3. Acquire [Cosmog](https://unboundwiki.com/pokemon/solgaleo/<https:/unboundwiki.com/pokemon/cosmog/>)

  
There are two ways that you can acquire Solgaleo in Pokemon Unbound, depending on what you do with your Cosmog.
### Method #1
The first and most straightforward is acquiring Cosmog and completely evolving it into Solgaleo.
Solgaleo evolves from Cosmoem at level 53 only during the day.
![Evolving Cosmeom into Solgaleo / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/01-pokemon-unbound-evolving-cosmeom-into-solgaleo.jpg)Evolving Cosmeom into Solgaleo
### Method #2
Those who evolved Cosmoem during the night to get Lunala instead can still get Solgaleo.
Return to Route 12’s beach where you initially found Cosmog and go north.
![Going north after reaching the Route 12 beach. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/02-pokemon-unbound-going-north-after-reaching-the-route-12-beach.jpg)Going north after reaching the Route 12 beach.
The portal with Solgaleo will be in the northern part of the beach. The portal only spawns if you have Lunala in your party.
![Finding the portal with Solgaleo. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/03-pokemon-unbound-finding-the-portal-with-solgaleo.jpg)Finding the portal with Solgaleo.
