Type | Fighting  
National Dex | #107  
Location | Evolve a Tyrogue at Level 20 with its Def stat greater than its Atk stat (Def > Atk)  
Abilities | Inner Focus | Prevents flinching.  
Iron Fist | Boosts punching moves.  
Unseen Fist | Contact moves bypass Protect.  
Egg Groups | Human Like  
Wild Held Items | None
