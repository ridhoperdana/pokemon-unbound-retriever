# Heatran
In: [Pokédex](https://unboundwiki.com/pokemon/heatran/<https:/unboundwiki.com/category/pokedex/>)
Heatran  
---  
![](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/sprites/pokemon/heatran.png)  
Type | Fire Steel  
National Dex | #485  
Location | In the middle of the room inside Cinder Volcano that’s flooded with lava. (Requires Magma Stone)  
Abilities | Flash Fire | Powers up if hit by fire.  
Flame Body | Burns the foe on contact.  
Egg Groups | Undiscovered  
Wild Held Items | None  
## Location
### (click to expand)
Prerequisites  
---  
  * Obtain the Magma Stone by completing the [“Master Incubator”](https://unboundwiki.com/pokemon/heatran/<https:/unboundwiki.com/missions/mission-039/>) side mission
  * Have [Rock Climb](https://unboundwiki.com/pokemon/heatran/<https:/unboundwiki.com/tms-hms/hm08-rock-climb/>)
  * Have [Surf](https://unboundwiki.com/pokemon/heatran/<https:/unboundwiki.com/tms-hms/hm03-surf/>)
  * Have a Fire-type Pokemon in your party

  
Completing the Master Incubator side mission rewards you with the Magma Stone, which is crucial to reaching Heatran.
![Obtaining the Magma Stone. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/01-pokemon-unbound-obtaining-the-magma-stone.jpg)Obtaining the Magma Stone.
With the Magma Stone, fly to Cinder Volcano’s entrance. Then from the entrance, walk south.
![Walking south from the main Cinder Volcano entrance. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/02-pokemon-unbound-walking-south-from-the-main-cinder-volcano-entrance.jpg)Walking south from the main Cinder Volcano entrance.
Walk past the bridge and continue on the narrow path east when the path south runs out.
![Following the narrow path going east. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/03-pokemon-unbound-following-the-narrow-path-going-east.jpg)Following the narrow path going east.
Use Rock Climb to descend and enter Cinder Volcano from the southeast entrance here.
![Entering Cinder Volcano’s southeast entrance. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/04-pokemon-unbound-entering-cinder-volcanos-southeast-entrance.jpg)Entering Cinder Volcano’s southeast entrance.
Once inside, continue following the linear path north.
![Following the path north. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/05-pokemon-unbound-following-the-path-north.jpg)Following the path north.
Go through the passageway west of the ladder.
![Going through the passageway west of the ladder. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/06-pokemon-unbound-going-through-the-passageway-west-of-the-ladder.jpg)Going through the passageway west of the ladder.
Use Surf to have your Fire-type Pokemon help you surf north on the lava.
![Using a Fire-type Pokemon to surf on the lava. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/07-pokemon-unbound-using-a-fire-type-pokemon-to-surf-on-the-lava.jpg)Using a Fire-type Pokemon to surf on the lava.
Heatran is on a rocky platform in the middle of the area.
![Finding Heatran in the middle of the lava. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/08-pokemon-unbound-finding-heatran-in-the-middle-of-the-lava.jpg)Finding Heatran in the middle of the lava.
