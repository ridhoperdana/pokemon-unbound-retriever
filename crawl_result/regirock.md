# Regirock
In: [Pokédex](https://unboundwiki.com/pokemon/regirock/<https:/unboundwiki.com/category/pokedex/>)
Regirock  
---  
![](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/sprites/pokemon/regirock.png)  
Type | Rock  
National Dex | #377  
Location | Inside the Desert Ruins located in the Great Desert  
Abilities | Clear Body | Prevents ability reduction.  
Sturdy | Negates 1-hit KO attacks.  
Egg Groups | Undiscovered  
Wild Held Items | None  
## Location
### (click to expand)
Prerequisites  
---  
  * Complete the [“Tomb Raider”](https://unboundwiki.com/pokemon/regirock/<https:/unboundwiki.com/missions/mission-076/>) side mission
  * Defeat the Elite Four to become Champion

  
Fly to the Gurun Town Pokemon Center and exit town going west, leading into the Great Desert.
![Going toward the Great Desert. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/01-pokemon-unbound-going-toward-the-great-desert.jpg)Going toward the Great Desert.
Continue west until you walk past a cactus below you. 
When you see the cactus, turn south to find the Desert Ruins – go inside.
![Entering the Desert Ruins. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/02-pokemon-unbound-entering-the-desert-ruins.jpg)Entering the Desert Ruins.
There’s a movable boulder puzzle you must solve inside.
![The boulder puzzle the player must solve. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/03-pokemon-unbound-the-boulder-puzzle-the-player-must-solve.jpg)The boulder puzzle the player must solve.
You’ll solve the puzzle by forming an “H” with the boulders using Strength, starting with the center boulder being the center of the “H”. 
You will hear a tingling sound every time you place a boulder in the right spot. You can reference the screenshot below for the solved puzzle solution. 
![Entering the newly created path after solving the puzzle. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/04-pokemon-unbound-entering-the-newly-created-path-after-solving-the-puzzle.jpg)Entering the newly created path after solving the puzzle.
Once it’s solved, the path to the north will open up.
Regirock is waiting inside the newly opened area.
![Finding Regirock. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/12/05-pokemon-unbound-finding-regirock.jpg)Finding Regirock.
