# Arceus
In: [Pokédex](https://unboundwiki.com/pokemon/arceus/<https:/unboundwiki.com/category/pokedex/>)
Arceus  
---  
![](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/sprites/pokemon/arceus.png)  
Type | Normal  
National Dex | #493  
Location | Dream Research Lab (Scientist NPC). Must first acquire all [Creation Plates](https://unboundwiki.com/pokemon/arceus/<https:/unboundwiki.com/misc-info/useful-npcs/plate-maker/>) from the Plate Maker at the Seaport City Shipyard.  
Abilities | Multitype | Changes type to match held Plate.  
Egg Groups | Undiscovered  
Wild Held Items | None  
## Location
### (click to expand)
Prerequisites  
---  
  * Complete [“The Endless Nightmare”](https://unboundwiki.com/pokemon/arceus/<https:/unboundwiki.com/missions/mission-066/>) side mission
  * Have all 17 types of Evolution Plates (via the [Plate Maker](https://unboundwiki.com/pokemon/arceus/<https:/unboundwiki.com/misc-info/useful-npcs/plate-maker/>))

  
Fly to Tarmigan Town. Then go south from the Tarmigan Town Pokemon Center and follow the path west.
![Going west from the Tarmigan Town Pokemon Center. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/11/01-pokemon-unbound-going-west-from-the-tarmigan-town-pokemon-center.jpg)Going west from the Tarmigan Town Pokemon Center.
Continue on the path until you eventually reach the Dream Research Lab.
![Entering the Dream Research Lab. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/11/02-pokemon-unbound-entering-the-dream-research-lab.jpg)Entering the Dream Research Lab.
Inside, approach the scientist NPC to the right of the Hypno.
![Approaching the scientist NPC. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/11/03-pokemon-unbound-approaching-the-scientist-npc.jpg)Approaching the scientist NPC.
As long as you’ve completed The Endless Nightmare mission, this scientist will claim to have created a device to send others into their nightmare. To use it, you must collect all 17 types of plates that can be dug up using the ADM or acquired from the [Plate Maker NPC](https://unboundwiki.com/pokemon/arceus/<https:/unboundwiki.com/misc-info/useful-npcs/plate-maker/>).
If you have all necessary plates then continue on.
![Asking the scientist to use the machine. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/11/04-pokemon-unbound-asking-the-scientist-to-use-the-machine.jpg)Asking the scientist to use the machine.
Approach Darkrai after being sent into your nightmare.
![Approaching the nightmare Darkrai. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/11/05-pokemon-unbound-approaching-the-nightmare-darkrai.jpg)Approaching the nightmare Darkrai.
The Darkrai will disappear, leaving a narrow set of stairs you should climb.
![Climbing the narrow set of stairs. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/11/06-pokemon-unbound-climbing-the-narrow-set-of-stairs.jpg)Climbing the narrow set of stairs.
Arceus is waiting for the player at the top.
![Finding Arceus at the top of the stairs. / Pokémon Unbound](https://static.unboundwiki.com/wp-content/assets/images/2024/11/07-pokemon-unbound-finding-arceus-at-the-top-of-the-stairs.jpg)Finding Arceus at the top of the stairs.
